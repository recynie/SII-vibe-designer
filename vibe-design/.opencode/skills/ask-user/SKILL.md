Interview user relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. 

Ask the questions one at a time.

Do not ask questions that can be answered by searching the Internet. Ask questions about the brief from user and clarify ambigous aims of user.

不要询问用户可能的事实（“官网网址是什么”“有没有logo地址”），而是询问需求（“你希望设计几种文创”“这个任务是否需要多个风格”）。
